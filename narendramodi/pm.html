Maan ki Baat

DETAILS

Mann Ki Baat' is a radio program of the Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi. Some special things about this program:


  • This program was first broadcast on October 3, 2014.

  • The 100th episode of 'Mann Ki Baat' was aired on 30 April 2023.

  • The aim of this programme is to interact with citizens on issues related to day-to-day governance.

  • In this programme, the Prime Minister addresses the citizens on issues of national importance and his views.

  • Akashvani (All India Radio) has been chosen to broadcast this programme to the public.

  • The objective of this program is to increase public awareness, public participation, and public welfare spirit.

  • The aim of this programme is to awaken the collective consciousness of more than 125 crore Indians and inspire them for nation building.
